Planet: Eiden

Welcome to mission control. You are now in control of your own space research centre. Take a minute to familiarize yourself with your new work station.
Your station monitoring device is split into 3 sections, your home screen, an expansion screen, and a map/radar device. Your home screen will display all current, relevant data to you, for example the number or ships you currently control. It also displays all potential procedures for you to choose from. It displays all messages being broadcast to you or your station, and gives alerts you to your current financial status, with incomes and outgoings.
The expansion screen is where you can expand your centre or its assets. There are more departments you can add, and ship upgrades as well.
Lastly the map will give you a geographical indication as to where your ships are when on missions. Some locations in the system are far more profitable than others, but you'll need to find that out for yourself.
